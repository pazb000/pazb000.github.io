Autumn is the season of change, with leaves transforming into a stunning display of red, orange, and yellow hues. The air turns crisp, making it ideal for cozy sweaters, hot beverages, and scenic drives to witness the fall foliage. It's also harvest season, bringing a bounty of fresh fruits and vegetables.

4 seasons are inevitable yet unpredictable!
